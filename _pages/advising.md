### Supervision Scope & Expertise
* **Research Methodology:** Guidance on experimental design, comparative benchmarking, and deep-learning pipeline optimization.
* **Technical Excellence:** Supervision of high-quality code development in **Python** (PyTorch, Open3D, 3D Slicer API) with a focus on open-science practices.
* **Scientific Communication:** Mentorship in drafting high-impact manuscripts, designing effective scientific visualizations, and preparing for international conferences.
* **Interdisciplinary Collaboration:** Facilitating co-supervision with clinical partners and engineering experts at **PMC**, **NKI-AVL**, **UT**, and *Erasmus MC*.

### Outcomes
Supervised projects are designed to bridge the gap between AI theory and clinical utility. Outcomes often include **validated software prototypes**, **clinical pilot evaluations**, and **peer-reviewed publications**, directly supporting the advancement of AI-assisted surgical navigation, omics analysis, and oncology.

If you are a student interested in pursuing a research internship or thesis within **AI-driven medical imaging, surgical robotics, or computer vision**, you are welcome to contact me at **m.khan@utwente.nl**.
